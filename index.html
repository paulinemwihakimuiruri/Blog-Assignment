COLORED AMIDST CHAOS

boy was brought to life. Existing as a colored kid
at the time was accompanied by discrimination and

survive in a world where you were either black or

REFLECTIVE BLOG

Gender Roles and Relationship Expectations.

From the quote above, in relation to the traditions of
our societies, it was expected of a husband to be in
control of his wife. In many communitiesthe husband would
dictate the way of living of his family. The wife, would
be tasked with staying at home, doing house chores and
taking care of the childen and abide by the rules set by
her husband. This was what Abel's family expected of Patricia
when they went to visit his family together with his son Andrew.
This kind of tradition was archaic and Patricia decided she
would never go to visit his family again.

In modern society, the gender roles are now evolving, moving
away from these traditions and leaning towards more diverse
and inclusive approaches.In politics and work places as well,
so many women have risen to the occassion, taking on authority
and responsibilities. They are allowed to handle family as well
as have careers. Men in modern society encourage their wives to
pursue their dreams and they show up to support them when needed.
Both men and women are being exposed to same opportunities without
gender discrimination. The notion of being a female is being
discarded and now everyone with the ability to take on a task is
considered equal regardless of gender.
