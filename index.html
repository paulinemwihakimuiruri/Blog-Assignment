Gender Roles and Relationship Expectations.

From the quote above, in relation to the traditions of our societies, it was expected of a husband to be in control of his wife. In many communities,
the husband would dictate the way of living of his family. The wife would be tasked with staying at home, doing house chores and taking care of the
childen and abide by the rules set by her husband. This was what Abel's family expected of Patricia when they went to visit his family together with
his son Andrew. This kind of tradition was archaic and Patricia decided she would never go to visit his family again.

In modern society, the gender roles are now evolving, moving away from these traditions and leaning towards more diverse and inclusive approaches. In
politics and work places as well, so many women have risen to the occassion, taking on authority and responsibilities. They are allowed to handle family
as well as have careers. Men in modern society encourage their wives to pursue their dreams and they show up to support them when needed. Both male and
female are being exposed to same opportunities without gender discrimination. The notion of being a female is being discarded and now everyone with the
ability to take on a task is considered equal regardless of gender.
